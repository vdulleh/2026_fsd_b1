It’s easy to confuse Agentic AI with Robotic Process Automation (RPA), which is technology that focuses on rules-based tasks performed on computers. The distinction lies in rigidity versus reasoning. RPA is excellent at following a strict script: if X happens, do Y. It mimics human hands. Agentic AI mimics human reasoning. It does not follow a linear script; it creates one. It can plan, make decisions, and take actions on behalf of users. It’s the difference between a self-driving car that follows pre-set routes and one that navigates complex traffic situations autonomously.
